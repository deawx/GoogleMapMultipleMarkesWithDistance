Distance and Travel Time to Multiple Locations

Find distances and travel times from your home to any number of locations at once. All the locations will be marked with pins along with the travel time and distance to each location from your home address. All the Locations will also be displayed in tabular format sorted according to the distance and travel time.

Doctor's North and East Transfer List 2017

Find distances and travel times from your home to Hospitals published in Sri Lanka Doctor's North and East Transfer List 2017. All the Hospitals will be marked with pins along with the travel time and distance to each Hospitals from your home address. All the Hospitals will also be displayed in tabular format sorted according to the distance and travel time.
